Privacy Policy

Extension Name: NonStop Youtube

Last updated: July 20, 2024

This privacy policy explains how this Chrome extension handles your information. The NonStop Youtube extension does not collect, use, or share any personal data from its users.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect any personal information or data from your device. It solely operates to cancel the "Still Watching" pop-up on YouTube without storing or transmitting any information.

Data Usage

As no data is collected, no data is used by this extension.

Data Sharing

As no data is collected, no data is shared with any third parties.
